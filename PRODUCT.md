# biyatrix — Product Context

## Product Purpose
AI-native social platform where humans dump raw thoughts, struggles, and lived experiences. AI organizes and routes those experiences to the exact person who needs them. Not a Q&A tool. Not a knowledge base. A brainstorm dump — a home for messy humans.

## Register
product

## Users
- Solo builders, indie hackers, small creators building in public
- People who feel alone in their struggle and want to find others like them
- Frustrated with polished social media that rewards performance over usefulness
- Mobile-first users, often on phones, often on the go
- Globally distributed — Tamil Nadu builders, rural problem-solvers, niche craftspeople

## Brand Tone
Raw. Real. Intimate. Slightly rebellious. Anti-polish. The opposite of LinkedIn.
Not corporate. Not startup-clean. Feels like a late-night voice note.

## Core Emotion
"Oh my god, someone else also thinks like that? I thought I was crazy."

## Anti-References
- LinkedIn (professional polish, performative)
- Product Hunt (clean startup aesthetic, growth-hacker vibes)
- Generic SaaS dashboards (white, teal, navy)
- Twitter/X (engagement bait, follower count obsession)
- Any design that looks like it was made with a template

## Three Core Actions
1. DUMP — speak or type raw thoughts, no polish needed
2. FIND — search for people who've been through the same struggle
3. FEED — see what others are dumping right now

## Visual Identity
- Logo: pixel art green butterfly (8-bit, intentionally retro)
- Palette: neon electric green on near-pure black — like a glowing butterfly in darkness
- Feel: late night, raw, slightly dangerous, alive
